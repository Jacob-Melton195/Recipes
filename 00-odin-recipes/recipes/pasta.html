pasta and sauce

Discription

Pasta is an Italian staple made from wheat flour and water, typically boiled and served with a sauce. It comes in many shapes like spaghetti, penne, and fettuccine. Common sauces include marinara, Alfredo, and pesto, making pasta a versatile and satisfying meal option.

How to Make Pasta

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add a generous amount of salt.
  2. Add the pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  3. Drain the pasta, reserving a little pasta water.
  4. Prepare the sauce:
    • In a pan, heat marinara, Alfredo, or pesto sauce.
  5. Toss the pasta with the sauce, using reserved pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce.
  6. Top with Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs if desired.
