Shell and stuff

Discription

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish made with folded or rolled tortillas filled with a variety of ingredients such as seasoned meat, lettuce, cheese, salsa, and sour cream. They’re quick to prepare and highly customizable, making them a popular street food and home-cooked favorite.

How to Make Tacos

  1. Cook the meat:
    • Brown ground beef, turkey, or chicken in a skillet.
    • Drain fat and stir in taco seasoning with a little water.
  2. Prepare the toppings:
    • Shred lettuce, dice tomatoes, grate cheese, and get salsa and sour cream ready.
  3. Warm the taco shells or tortillas.
  4. Assemble the tacos:
    • Fill each shell with meat and your favorite toppings.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
